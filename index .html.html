Safety Architecture

6 layers of protection — not an afterthought

Every feature is designed around one belief: if women trust it, everyone trusts it.

🛡️

Identity Verification

Aadhaar + live selfie AI matching. One account per real person. Criminals permanently blocked.

🆘

One-Tap SOS

Instantly alerts police (112), emergency contacts with live GPS, and our 24/7 safety team. Auto audio recording begins.

📍

Live Location Sharing

Share real-time location with trusted contacts during the event. Geo-fence alerts if you move unexpectedly.

👩

Women Safety Suite

Shake-to-Alert, buddy system, women-only events, gender ratio filters, secret exit code words.

⭐

Trust Score System

0–100 score per user. Good behavior unlocks better events. Bad behavior means bans — no second chances for serious offenses.

Accountability Tiers

Rude behavior → warning. Harassment → 7-day ban. Assault → permanent ban + auto FIR filed with police.
